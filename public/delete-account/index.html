Delete Account – Nutrimetrics

Account deletion guide and information

⚠️

WARNING – IRREVERSIBLE ACTION

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. All your personal data will be permanently deleted from our servers and cannot be recovered.

📱

HOW TO DELETE YOUR ACCOUNT

1

Open the Nutrimetrics app on your mobile device

2

Tap on the "Settings" tab at the bottom of the screen

3

Scroll down to the "Security" section

4

Tap on "Delete Account" (red button)

5

Read the warning and tap "Delete" to confirm

⏱️ The process takes a few seconds. You will be automatically logged out and redirected to the home screen once completed.

🗑️

PERMANENTLY DELETED DATA

Your account and all associated data will be completely deleted:

  • •User profile (name, email, date of birth, gender)
  • •Health data (weight, height, nutritional goals)
  • •Meal photos (all images stored in our cloud)
  • •Food journal (complete meal history)
  • •Physical activities (calories burned, exercises)
  • •Daily statistics (calories, macros, progress)
  • •Notification settings
  • •Push notification tokens
  • •Weight history
  • •Authentication data (Supabase account)
  • •All local application data

🔒 AI usage logs are anonymized (user ID is removed but technical metrics are kept to improve the service).

💾

RETAINED DATA

  • •Anonymized technical metrics (without user identifier) to improve the service
  • •Deletion audit logs (for security and compliance reasons)

No personally identifiable data is retained after deletion.

📞

NEED HELP?

If you encounter difficulties deleting your account:

Email: support@nutrimetrics.app

Subject: "Account deletion request"

Response: Within 24-48 hours

Our team can help you delete your account manually if necessary.
